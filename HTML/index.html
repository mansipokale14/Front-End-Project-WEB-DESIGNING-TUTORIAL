          An interview is a formal conversation between two or more people, 
          often used for hiring, journalism, or research purposes.
          Its primary goal is to gather information, assess qualifications, 
          and establish a connection between the participants. In a hiring
          context, an interview typically involves a series of questions 
          designed to evaluate an applicant's skills, experience, and fit 
          for a particular job. Journalistic interviews, on the other hand, 
          are used to gather information and quotes for news articles. 
          Preparing for an interview, including researching the company and 
          practicing common questions, can greatly improve the outcome.
